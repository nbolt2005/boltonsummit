Quick Drills

Do these when you feel stuck. Simple, repeatable, effective.

Drill 01 — Intent

Set direction before effort.

  • Who am I?
  • What do I want?
  • One sentence goal for the next 30 days.
Clarity creates velocity.

Drill 02 — Ask

One message that moves something forward.

  • Ask for feedback.
  • Ask for an intro.
  • Ask for a chance to help.
"You don't ask, you don't get."

Drill 03 — Momentum

No giant plans. One step.

  • Pick the smallest next action.
  • Do it in 15 minutes.
  • Stack the next step after.
Never underestimate a step forward.
