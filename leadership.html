Field Studies Club

Student-run outdoor organization

What it is

Field Studies is an outdoor club at Cal Poly that leads trips across California and beyond. I joined when it had about 50 members. Today it's a multicampus nonprofit with 700+ students.

How it works

Students build and lead everything. Trips run nearly every weekend - from local hikes to multi-day expeditions.

My arc

I started as a quiet freshman with shared ideas in a notebook. Those ideas became reality.

What I built

Systems, mentorship, structure that scales.

Club Structure

Trip systems

Behind every good trip is structure. Created flows that cover logistics, safety, food, transport, communication, and leader prep — so great trips can scale without losing the authenticity.
